Frequently Asked Questions

Nefaa Services offers an online platform for simplifying processes like renewing driving licenses, registering vehicles, and more in Lebanon.

To apply for a driving license, visit the 'Apply for Driving License' page and fill in the necessary details and required documents.

To renew your driving license, you will need your current license, ID proof, and a recent passport-size photo. For additional documents, check our 'Renew License' section.

You can contact us via our 'Contact' page or send an email to support@nefaaservices.com.

The fees for our services vary depending on the type of service. Please refer to the respective service page for detailed pricing information.
